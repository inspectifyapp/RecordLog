About RecordLog

Document anything. Keep a clear record.

What RecordLog is for

RecordLog is a camera app with GPS tagging, server timestamps, SHA-256 photo hashing, and PDF export. Use it to document any asset — property, vehicle, equipment, or site condition. Open the app, start a project, take your photos, and generate a clean PDF ready to save or share. No technical knowledge needed.

What's included

📄

Clean PDF Export

Generate a structured, organised PDF with all your photos, labels, and notes — ready to save or share instantly.

📂

Organised by Area

Group photos by room, area, or section. Drag to reorder. Every project stays neat and easy to read.

📍

GPS Tagging

Optional. Records your live device coordinates per photo, anchored to the project address.

🕐

Server Timestamps

Optional. Time recorded server-side via Firebase, not your device clock, for a consistent metadata record.

🔒

Locked Records

Once generated, your inspection is locked and stored in Firebase. The record stays intact as created.

🔐

SHA-256 Hashing

Each photo is fingerprinted with a unique hash so the integrity of the file can be confirmed.

Who uses RecordLog

Anyone who needs a clear, dated photo record with notes — properties, vehicles, equipment, medical screens, site conditions, and more.

How it works

1

Create a project

Enter the address of the property or asset. Give the project a name, or let RecordLog generate one automatically.

2

Take photos in-app

Use the RecordLog camera. Every photo is captured with metadata recorded at that exact moment — GPS and timestamp where available.

3

Add notes and labels

Title each photo, assign it to an area or room, and add optional notes up to 300 characters.

4

Review and reorder

Review all photos grouped by area before generating your report. Drag to reorder. Edit any detail.

5

Generate and export

Tap Generate PDF. RecordLog compiles your photos into a clean, organised PDF — locked, watermarked, and ready to download or share.

Start documenting today

Free to use. No technical knowledge required. Your first inspection takes less than five minutes.
