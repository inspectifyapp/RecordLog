Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 June 2026

RecordLog is a documentation app designed to help users capture photos, add notes, and export PDF reports. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using RecordLog.

Information you create

RecordLog may allow you to create project names, photos, notes, timestamps, and PDF reports. This information is created by you as part of your documentation workflow.

Local and app-based storage

Records and related content may be stored on your device or within app-related services required for the app to function. You are responsible for reviewing the information you enter and export.

Photos and metadata

RecordLog may use photos and optional metadata only to support documentation features selected by the user. Metadata use depends on app settings and user permission choices.

PDF exports

PDF reports are created from information entered or captured by the user. You control how exported PDF files are stored, shared, or distributed outside the app.

Support contact

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. This information is used only to respond to your support request.

No certification or verification

RecordLog does not inspect, certify, verify, or validate the accuracy of user-created records. You perform the inspection. RecordLog helps document what was observed.
